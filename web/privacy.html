Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how URL Switch ("we", "our", or "us") handles user data in our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Data Collection and Usage

URL Switch is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what you should know about our data practices:

What We Collect

  • URL Conversion Rules: We store the URL conversion rules you create, which may include regular expressions for matching and transforming URLs.
  • Extension Settings: We store your preferences such as enabled/disabled status of rule groups and auto-redirect settings.

How We Use Your Data

  • Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of URL Switch: converting URLs between related websites based on your defined rules.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry features are included in the extension.

Where Your Data is Stored

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's Storage API.
  • If you're signed into Chrome with sync enabled, your settings and rules may be synchronized across your devices through Chrome's built-in sync functionality.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not transmit your data to our servers.
  • We do not share your data with any third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can view all rules and settings in the extension's options page.
  • You can delete rules or reset the extension to remove all stored data.
  • You can export your rules to a local file and import them as needed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our GitHub repository and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please create an issue in our GitHub repository.

Compliance Statement

URL Switch's data collection and usage practices comply with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the extension's core functionality.

URL Switch is an open-source Chrome extension for bidirectional URL conversion.
